Shows all US states with visitor metrics, engagement data, and traffic distribution in a data table format.

## Predefined Filter

This report applies a predefined filter to show only US states:

| Filter | Value |
|--------|-------|
| `country_code` | US |

This filter is applied automatically and cannot be changed by the user.
